Our Story

Founded in a small Parisian atelier in 1985, Luxe Tailor began as a dream to revive the art of bespoke tailoring. Our founder, Jean-Luc Moreau, envisioned a space where craftsmanship and personal expression would intertwine to create garments that tell stories.

What started as a one-man workshop has grown into an internationally recognized tailoring house, yet we've never lost sight of our roots. Each garment we create carries the same attention to detail and commitment to quality that Jean-Luc instilled in his very first creations.

Today, with ateliers in Paris, Milan, and New York, we continue to honor traditional techniques while embracing innovation. Our team of master tailors combines decades of experience with fresh perspectives to create clothing that stands the test of time.
